How to Play

  1. Touch canvas to move on mobile. On PC use ← / → or A / D.
  2. Collect Life (green +) to gain 1 life; rotates slowly.
  3. Collect Shield (gold badge) to gain a shield that blocks one hit.
  4. Bomb clears some enemies when collected.
  5. Avoid falling enemies — reach high score to level up.
